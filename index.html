Biography

Kevin De Broyer (Halle, April 1st, 1993) is a Belgian Artist, he is mostly known as a landscape painter and colorist. He currently lives and works in Blankenberge and Ghent. He's born into a family with 1 sister and 4 brothers and a lovely mother called Marleen. He first started painting when he joined the academy of fine arts in Bruges where he graduated in 2015, and joined the Luca school of arts in 2016 where he is still studying.

His works consists mostly out of realistic paintings with abstract elements but he never loses the realistic parts, Kevin explained " For me a good abstract painter needs maturity, I'm not ready yet. For me a landscape is a medium to show my emotions and love of color without going full abstract".

His work is inspired by lots of artists mostly the Impressionists and abstract expressionists but also painters like Richard Diebenkorn, Wayne Thiebaud and David Hockney.
